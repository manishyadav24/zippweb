HOW IT WORKS

We provide complete end to end services from planning Bill of Materials (BOM), delivery schedules to inventory management at site. Our one-stop SCaaS system ensures contractors can seamlessly collaborate real-time across locations and transparently plan and manage their supply needs.

Step 01

Develop Bill of Materials

Contractors can share a detailed project BoM using our ready to use templates for different construction projects. Our team of experience engineers will help you refine your BoM to optimize costs and execution timelines.

Step 02

Develop detailed delivery schedules

Contractors can develop a detailed supply delivery schedule using our automated, real-time collaboration tools. Each delivery schedule is linked to clear execution milestones to ensure proper deliveries.

Step 03

Supplier planning

ZippMat works in the background with its extensive supplier network to develop the most optimized delivery plan and overall costing. Suppliers use ZippMat's online collaboration tool to optimize their production plans and share their quotes transparently.

Step 04

Online quotation

ZippMat will integrate all information to provide an optimized price quotation to our customers to minimize costs and reduce execution delays. Our customers can place orders using our automated inward and outward PO management system.

Step 05

Delivery planning

ZippMat app helps customers track execution milestones to ensure supply delivery plans are real-time updated to reflect on ground progress and prevent stock-outs or inventory pile-up at site.

Step 06

Automated POs

ZippMat alerts customers to place timely orders based on project site needs and execution milestones. Order placement is managed through an automated PO system.

Step 07

Track deliveries

Customers can see and manage real time delivery status of their orders on the ZippMat app.

Step 08

Site inventory management

Customers can manage real-time inventory levels across sites on ZippMat’s app. The inventory management system helps customers optimize inventory levels not just at a site level but across production sites on a real-time basis.

Step 09

Documentation store

An online document locker for customers to store and manage all relevant documents including invoices, delivery receipts, manuals, warranty documentation, and more.
